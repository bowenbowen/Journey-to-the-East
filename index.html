Growing up in China, a country that shares a lot of cultural roots with Japan and Korea, I didn’t dive into a completely unknown adventure when travelling in these two countries. However, my impressions of them were also proven superficial after the experience. This website acts as a journal and reflection of this journey in relation to my cultural background, documenting some of my interesting findings and previous misconceptions that were later found untrue.

Although I appear to be bad mouthing sometimes, I don’t have any negative intentions! I absolutely enjoyed the time and will come back again in the future.
